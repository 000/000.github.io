Introduction to the Artifact

The image you provided depicts "Nor Raed" (Rhinoceros Horn), a substance revered in Thai beliefs as one of the most potent natural amulets ("Krueng Rang"). Specifically identified as a personal sacred object of Phra Kru Wilas Kanjanatham (Wat Tha Khanun), it acts as a crucial ingredient in the legendary Ya Wasana Jindamanee medicine.

Beyond its material rarity, traditional folklore ascribes to it a unique combination of spiritual authorities: it is at once a weapon of command, a shield against curses, and a magnet for prosperity.

Unique Among Talismans

While many Thai amulets offer protection ("Kong Grapan"), the Nor Raed is distinct for its specific ability known as "Gae Athan" (Undo Black Magic).

Unlike Tiger teeth (pure power) or Ivory (purity/status), the Rhinoceros horn is viewed as a "Living Antidote." Folklore states that if dipped in water, it can detect poison or turn the water into holy water ("Nam Mon") to wash away bad luck.
