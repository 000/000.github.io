Cosine Similarity Calculation

similarity = cos(θ) = (A · B) / (||A|| × ||B||)
Example: Zen-AI-Pentest
mr7.ai · zen = 5(1) + 4(0) + 5(4) + 5(2) + 5(3) + 5(3) = 65
||mr7|| = √141 ≈ 11.87
||zen|| = √39 ≈ 6.245
similarity = 65 / (11.87 × 6.245) ≈ 0.877

Why Cosine Similarity?

Captures pattern alignment independent of magnitude, enabling comparison of platforms with different absolute capability levels but similar capability profiles.
