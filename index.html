Due to the nature of freelance data engineering services, refunds are not typically offered. Cancellation Policy

Clients may cancel a project at any time. If cancellation occurs before the project starts, no fees will be charged. If cancellation occurs after work has begun, a cancellation fee equivalent to the work completed up to that point will be charged. Please provide at least 48 hours notice for cancellations to minimize disruption.
